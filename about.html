About D&T

D&T Tree Surgery is Based in West Sussex and was formed by Jon Thwaites an Anthony Duffield over 10 years ago.


D&T are one of the leading and best firms in the area; providing a highly professional, friendly, efficient and affordable service through fully qualified and experienced arborists/tree surgeons and fencers with a wide range of skills.

We are a dedicated, caring and conscientious team with an ever-growing database of satisfied customers - both domestic and commercial. With a 99% feedback on Checkatrade and a team of vastly experienced proffesionals, no job is too big for D&T.
